DNA Day

To better promote the concept of the International Genetically Engineered Machine (iGEM) competition and our project "Ergothioneine Production Enhancement in Saccharomyces cerevisiae", we are conducting offline outreach activities in the central campus area in conjunction with DNA Day to educate people about the important scientific advances in genomics and how they affect our lives.

DNA Day, celebrated annually on April 25th, commemorates the successful completion of the Human Genome Project and the discovery of the double helix structure of DNA, which was published in 1953. The goal of International DNA Day is to educate everyday people about the importance of genomics so that they can make more informed decisions about their healthcare and even potential careers.

On this commemorative day, we prepared random quizzes and raffles to educate people about biology, and they were very enthusiastic to participate and get a small gift through the event. Our team members actively participated in the event and put a lot of enthusiasm and effort into promoting the event and the project, actively promoting the event on campus and guiding students to our booth to participate in our activities. This event laid a good foundation for the team's social practice and made more and more people know about synthetic biology.

Let ergothioneine come into life

On July 11th, iGEM team of Hubei University of Technology entered Hugong community for publicity, and the publicity activities were held in the mass service center of Party members. The activities included public lectures, poster publicity and answering questions, and the participants included iGEM team, instructors and community members.

Ergothioneine exists in many animals and plants, can be obtained through food, has brightening, anti-aging and anti-radiation effects, and can be used as health products or food additives. Today, iGEM team of Hubei University of Technology is committed to manufacturing high-quality ergothioneine through experimental means.

Let ergothione become well known. In fact, it widely exists in nature. The purpose of this activity is to offer free popular science to community residents through the exhibition window, to win a good spread in the community, and to reflect the social responsibility and the positive attitude towards ergothione research.

Over the years, Angel Yeast and Hubei University of Technology have carried out in-depth cooperation in scientific research, personnel training, employment and internship of college students, equipment resource sharing, product development and other aspects, which has produced fruitful results.
Yanlin Propaganda

In order to help people understand the main idea and competition process of iGEM, answer students' questions about iGEM and improve their professional quality, we held an iGEM information session on April 9th.

Members of the iGEM team led the presentation. At the beginning, we made a brief introduction to the International Genetic Engineering Machine Competition, so that students had a preliminary understanding of the competition. After that, through the explanation of the competition process, the competition content and their gains and growth during this period, the students had a relatively complete cognitive framework of the competition, and encouraged the students to actively participate in the competition through the domestic development status of iGEM and the awards of Hubei University of Technology in recent years.

Then we gave a general introduction to synthetic biology to the students. By explaining the introduction and definition of synthetic biology, the students had a preliminary understanding of synthetic biology, the research of artificial biological system, and explained the theoretical background and development process of synthetic biology, which aroused the students' strong interest. Let the students understand the strategic significance of synthetic biology to human society and realistic strategic significance. Finally, we warmly encourage them to actively participate in the contest and recommend them to follow the latest news of the contest on iGEM WeChat official account.

The publicity meeting was successful. Our explanation not only made the students who were interested in the competition understand the basic content of the competition, but also further improved their interest in this kind of competition.
